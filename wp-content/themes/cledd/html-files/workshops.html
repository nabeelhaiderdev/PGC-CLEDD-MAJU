TRAININGS & WORKSHOPS

5a

DECISION MAKING TECHNIQUES

These range from techniques for setting the scene for effective decision making, through tools that help you choose between different options, to skills for deciding whether to run a project or not. You can also learn how to make sound financial choices, and make good decisions.

5b

TEAM MOTIVATION & MOTIVATION IN ORGANIZATIONS

Can anyone directly motivate another individual, or do we motivate ourselves? Motivating Team Members helps managers learn the four stages to influence a team member to perform a task, while creating a work environment that will motivate higher performance.

5c

THE POWER OF POSITIVE THINKING AT WORKPLACE

The Power of Positive Thinking course starts by understanding how negative thinking in its many guises creeps into both individuals and organisations. It then focuses on how to challenge negative thoughts and then how to replace them with positive thoughts that can be sustained.

5d

INTERNAL CUSTOMER SERVICE & CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIPS

The Service Excellence Series of full and half-day customer service workshops focus on the critical role individuals play in building customer loyalty and creating positive service experiences.

5e

TEAM BUILDING & TEAM DEVELOPMENT

High-performing teams exhibit accountability, purpose, cohesiveness, and collaboration. How do you turn a dysfunctional group into a productive team? Can you make a good team better? Find out the answers to both questions during a full schedule of active team-building training.

5f

SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT THRIVING IN THE INTERNET 2.0

Whether you’re a new social media manager, an executive or an entry-level employee, this training course includes everything you need to know to come up to speed on social media management strategy and tactics.

5g

FUNDRAISING TECHNIQUES FOR NGOS

Asking for money is as scary for people as death and public speaking. But it doesn’t have to be Learn a simple method that takes much of the fear out of the process. This Training is perfect for nonprofit employees and volunteers and will give you proven techniques to improve your fundraising skills.

5h

ART OF SELLING, INFLUENCING & NEGOTIATING

Is objections create pressure to discuss things?
This training will help thousands of salespeople identify how to boost their performance and improve relations with customers.

5j

STRATEGIC VISION

This illuminating training provides an introduction to creating powerful visions. Identifying a clear and meaningful vision is critical towards personal and organizational success and excellence. This training will give you the tools you need for developing a worthy, powerful vision

5k

STRATEGIC TIME MANAGEMENT

The Strategic Time Management intensive workshop is designed to discuss modern cutting edge concepts and techniques to bring effectiveness and efficiency to balance the work. This workshop will help reduce stress level and get control over your professional life.

5l

PRESENTATION SKILLS FOR MANAGERS

Presenting information clearly and effectively is a key skill to get your message or opinion across and, today, presentation skills are required in almost every field.

5m

BUSINESS COMMUNICATION & INTERPERSONAL COMMUNICATION

You have come to the right place if you’re looking to improve your communication skills from developing your interpersonal and presentation skills to perfecting your professional writing techniques.

5n

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

This training has been designed to equip you with the skills needed for today’s diverse and demanding requirements in modern project management. Successful project management today involves increased demand for quality, delivery on time, strict adherence to budget and required regulatory controls.

5o

MAKING WORKPLACE AS HOME

We provide training in areas involving change and interpersonal skills within the workplace. Our training style is strongly interactive and pragmatic, incorporating current workplace experiences and trends

5p

LEADERSHIP THROUGH CHARACTERS

Our Leadership Development and Training programs push each and every delegate to gain a rigorous personal insight into what makes them tick and then, with that understanding of themselves, to stretch their ‘comfort zones’ into new and challenging territory.

5q

PERSONAL & PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Whether you want to improve your performance in Sales, Leadership, Interpersonal Skills, Communications, Presentations or Customer Service, personal and professional development that perfectly match your learning style preference.
