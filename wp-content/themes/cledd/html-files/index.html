Mohammad Ali Jinnah University

About

Mohammad Ali Jinnah University (MAJU) chartered by the Government of Sindh and recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC), was established in 1998. The university is owned by the highly reputable Punjab Group of Colleges and over a span of 33 years, the group has grown into the largest educational network in Pakistan providing top quality education with a student body of over 490,000. The Group also proudly owns Dunya TV which is one of the largest and most viewed television networks in the country. Mohammad Ali Jinnah University along with its strong academic credentials and services has been honoured with the 1st position in the General University category by Sindh HEC through CIEC.

Center for Executive Learning Development & Diversity (CELDD)

About

Having our core belief of “Live, Learn & Be Inspired,” Center for Executive Learning Development & Diversity (CELDD) was established to challenge, encourage and transform the way businesses and individuals think. CELDD blends different kinds of learning experiences, tailored to personal and team objectives and focuses on the practical application of skills and capabilities in the workplace. CELDD recognizes the need for training and developing innovative leaders and organizations to compete in today’s unpredictable economy, regardless of industry scope or size. At CELDD, we understand what it takes to earn organization’s trust and expectations, hence we offer various self-developed and customized trainings and workshops to address and overcome an organization’s challenges and enhance its revenue potential.
