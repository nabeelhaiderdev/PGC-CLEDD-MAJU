CUSTOMIZED TRAININGS

Center for Executive Learning Development & Diversity (CELDD) offers its corporate organizations a unique approach to the training expertise. To get desired results and get highest value addition from the training, we provide organizations with Customized Trainings. In the Customized Training, our training & development effort extends beyond the training room. We work in partnership with our client:

  • Understand your business and development needs and analyses the situation at hand.
  • Design customized high impact training interventions to cater to the particular situation company is facing.

These customized trainings will not only cater to the situation at hand, but will also equip the employees to cater to such situations in future effectively.
